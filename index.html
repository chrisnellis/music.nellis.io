Yearly Spotify Countdown Archive

Starting in 2015, I've celebrated the end of the year by creating a playlist comprised of my favorite songs released in that year. This page is intended to include all of my countdowns for easy access.

All playlists are in countdown order such that the playlist starts with the lowest rank song of the year going to the #1 song at the end.

Thanks for visiting!!
